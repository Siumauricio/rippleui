## Inspiration

It's great to have inspiration for your projects, and these UI frameworks can be a great source of inspiration for your own designs. NextUI, GeistUI, and Vuesax all have their own unique features and design approaches that can inspire new ideas and approaches to your own UI design work. For example, NextUI's focus on responsive design can inspire you to prioritize mobile-friendliness in your own designs and modern components. GeistUI's modular approach can inspire you to think about breaking your designs down into smaller, reusable components. And Vuesax's clean and elegant design language can inspire you to strive for simplicity and clarity in your own designs. Overall, these frameworks can serve as a source of inspiration for your own UI design work, helping you to create beautiful and functional user interfaces that meet the needs of your users.

RippleUI Docs was inspired by the following UI frameworks:

- [NextUI](https://nextui.org/)
- [GeistUI](https://geist-ui.dev/)
- [Vuesax](https://vuesax.com/)

RippleUI Pallete and colors was inspired by the following color palettes:

- [NextUI](https://nextui.org/docs/theme/default-theme#base)
